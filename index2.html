YOU&MEOW. Bristol's Cat Café

Being at university can be challenging for many reasons, one of which is being away from your pets. One way to relax from coursework and revision is to go and visit the ‘You&Meow’ Cat Café down by harbourside. Best to book as this is a popular destination! Relax for an hour with the adorable felines whilst drinking a cup of tea.
Enjoy them by yourself or with friends!
